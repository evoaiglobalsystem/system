Field Definitions
Lead ID
Auto-generated unique identifier for each lead record.
Source
Where the lead originated — Website, LinkedIn, Referral, etc.
Page / URL
The page the lead submitted from (e.g. /services).
Deal Value
Estimated deal size in USD — contributes up to +20 pts to the lead score.
Follow-up Date
Scheduled next-contact date. Overdue dates appear highlighted in the sidebar.
Comments
Internal notes visible only to your team — not sent to the lead.
Pro Tips
🔴
Score increases automatically as you fill in more fields and advance the pipeline stage — no manual input needed.
⏰
Overdue filter: Click "Follow Up Dues" in the sidebar to instantly see all leads with past follow-up dates.
💰
Pipeline Value in the stats bar counts all open deals (excluding Won/Lost).
🔄
Board view for pipeline: Drag cards between columns to instantly update a lead's stage — score updates on the next load.
📥
Export CSV includes all fields including follow-up date, deal value, and source for offline analysis.
